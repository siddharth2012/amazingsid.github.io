mystery of the secret lab

2021-06-25 246 words 2 minutes
Contents

It was windy at Black Hills. The moon rose up high in the cloudless sky. Sid was up doing research for his boss Metros.
Right then the telephone rang, it was a call from the Black Hills’s counsel. “Hello, who is this”, Sid asked answering the telephone. “Steve from the counsil of Black Hills” answered from the otherside of the tellephone. “Sid, you have a mission to keep your eye on, There is another spy who calls himself “Jack” has decided to put poison in the whole town and we will have do anything that he says after he drops it”, Steve said with fear in his voice. “Got to run, I have to hide because Jack is here”, Steve said.

Sid dropped the phone and made a run for the door. He put on his ninja suit and ran out of the house as quickly as he could. He reached where Steve was. But he could not find Jack. Sid used his iPad to track Jack. The iPad showed the Jack was under ground beneath him. Sid kicked the ground to see if there was a door. When he kicked on a spot, the grass came off.

There it was a metal door, Sid opened it and jumped in. Sid saw Jack working on the poison. Sid ran and snatched the poison off Jack and threw it on him. Then Sid tied him to a chair and called the police.

That is another mission done.
