INVENTIONS

BENJAMIN FANKLIN

Benjamin Franklin was born on January 17th 1706 in Boston, Massachusetts. He first became known to the public as the publisher of the newspaper, The Pennsylvania Gazette. Did you know that Benjamin Franklin was one of the five members that drafted the declaration of independence? Perhaps he is most famous for work with electricity. His experiments helped prove that lightning is a form of electricity. Franklin invented the lighting rod which helped buildings to keep safe from lightning. His other inventions included bifocals, carriage odometers, the franklin stove and the glass harmonica.

THOMAS EDISON

Thomas Edison was one of the greatest inventors and created hundreds of new things. Some of his most famous invention were the phonograph, the first machine that could record and play back sound and the light bulb. He also started the company general electricity which is still the largest company today.

ELI WITNEY

Eli Witney invented the cotton gin in 1793. It was a machine that could harvest cotton very quickly. Gin stands for engine. Before this invention it would take hours of work to lean and harvest cotton. His invention allowed Americans to make lots of money.

MARIE CURIE

Marie Curie was born on November 7th 1867 in Warsaw, Poland. Marie learned how to read and write early on since both of her parents were teachers. She was a very bright child who had impeccable memories. Even though Marie grew up at a time when only men attended college, she worked hard to pay for her sister to attend a special collage in France that allowed woman to attend.

ALEXANDER GRAHAM BELL

Alexander Graham Bell is a very famous inventor. He invented lots of things and also the telephone. Alexander was given his middle name ‘’Graham’’ when he was 10 years old. Alexander started inventing the telephone in 1847. To invent the telephone Alexander had to work with his closest friend Thomas Watson.

The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. The telephone helps us to speak to someone who is far away.

WRIGHT BROTHERS

The plane was invented by Wright Brothers. The plane helps us to travel long distance in short time.

THOMAS EDISON

Thomas Edison invented light bulb. The light bulb helps us to see in the dark.

CARL BENZ

Carl Benz invited the car. The car helps us to travel from one place to another

CHARLES BABBAGE

The computer was invented by Charles Babbage. The computer helps us to learn and make work easy.
