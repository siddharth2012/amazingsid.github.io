The mean crab and the smart snail

A story about a crab and snail and how mean behaviour can put you into trouble

2021-07-10 283 words 2 minutes
Contents
    • MORLE: DO NOT TEASE PEOPLE FOR WHAT THEIR PERSONALITYS ARE.

It was a nice weather at the county side. The sun was bright and the air was cool and light.

The snail came out of his shell for a walk. While he was slithering through the big beach rocks, he went across crab’s house. Crab poked his long thin eyes out of the pitch black hole. “Hello crab”, said snail moving one inch closer to crab’s house, leaving a silvery trial. “Hi snail, would you mind if I went for a walk with you?", crab asked in a rough voice. “Of course, I don’t mind if you join me”, said snail. “Why don’t we go to the beach side and get some fresh air”, said the snail, already walking ahead of the crab.

After a while when snail and crab walked for a mile they went under a shady tree. Crab picked a fruit out of the tree with gee. “You have a soft body you don’t have any claws to pick up our food you are useless” crab said scraping a seed. Upset by the crab’s manners snail turned his back to crab. Noticing crab walking towards a puddle “Hey, crab lookout you are going to step in a puddle”, warned the snail. “You are useless why listen to you”, crab said and turned away from the puddle.

Soon snail saw there was an eagle flying around crab’s back. “LOOK OUT CRAB!” snail exclaimed. Snail climbed up a tree and tucked his head into his shell. “Ha ha very funny joke snail”, crab teased and walked away. The eagle saw the crab, swooped and caught him in his feet and flew away.

MORLE: DO NOT TEASE PEOPLE FOR WHAT THEIR PERSONALITYS ARE.
